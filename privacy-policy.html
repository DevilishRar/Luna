Privacy Policy

Service Data Logging

Luna Serverside acknowledges the importance of your data and does its best at ensuring your data is securely stored in our systems. Luna will never sell or provide your user information or share it in any way, we respect your privacy. Below listed are some of the things we may log to ensure our service remains running smoothly for you and others.

User Identifiers

User identifiers categorizing your Discord account, your ROBLOX account, current session, and your device.

HTTP Requests

HTTP requests sent to or through our service (including both encrypted and decrypted forms of requests).

Discord Actions

Information on the actions done by your Discord Account on our server including messages sent, bot interactions, etc.

Executed Scripts

Any scripts executed through the use of our service including the Javascript code.

Game Access

The games you specifically access and use our services to execute scripts or commands through.

Discord Data

Discord ID, names, nicknames, and profile picture(s).

We respect your privacy and data and if you'd like your data erased from our systems at any time, please open a support ticket in our Discord Server.
